## Common Questions

**Q: What happened to "Constitutional AI"?**  
A: We rebranded. "Constitutional AI" is Anthropic's term. MIF (Mutual Intelligence Framework) is our distinct approach emphasizing co-development and co-stewardship.

**Q: What's the relationship between MIF, ELEANOR, RBJA, and EJC?**  
A: MIF is the philosophy. ELEANOR is the system design. RBJA is the technical specification. EJC is the reference code.

**Q: Can I deploy this in production?**  
A: Yes! Follow the 30-day sprint plan. Start in shadow mode, move to advisory, then enforcement.

**Q: What about the EJE code on GitHub?**  
A: It will be renamed to EJC (Ethical Jurisprudence Core). The redirect will work.

**Q: Is this really different from Anthropic's Constitutional AI?**  
A: Yes! Training-time vs. decision-time, monolithic vs. multi-critic, research vs. production-ready.
